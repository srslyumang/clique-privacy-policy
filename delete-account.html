How to Delete Your Clique Account

Clique makes it easy to delete your account directly from within the app. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Open the Clique App

Launch the Clique app on your mobile device and make sure you're logged in to your account.

Step 2: Go to Your Profile

Tap on the profile icon (face icon) in the top right corner of the screen to access your profile.

Step 3: Access Account Settings

Once in your profile view, tap on the power icon in the top right corner to access account options.

Step 4: Select "Delete Account"

In the account options menu, tap on "Delete account" (shown in yellow/gold text).

Step 5: Confirm Deletion

A confirmation dialog will appear asking if you're sure you want to delete your account. Tap "Yes, delete account" to proceed.

Important Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your account data, including your profile information, matches, and messages will be permanently deleted.

If you've recently changed your password, you may need to log out and log back in before deleting your account.

What Happens After Deletion?

Once you confirm deletion:

  • Your account will be immediately deleted from our system
  • Your profile will no longer be visible to other users
  • All your personal data associated with the account will be removed from our database
  • You will be logged out and redirected to the app's welcome screen

Need Help?

If you're experiencing any issues with account deletion or have questions about your data, please contact our support team at support@clique-app.com.
